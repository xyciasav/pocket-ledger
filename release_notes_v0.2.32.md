Pocket Ledger v0.2.32 Qt Preview

- Adds cash account roles: Bills checking, Spending checking, Savings, and Other.
- Adds Settings management for cash accounts.
- Keeps the Cashflow forecast anchored to the Bills checking account.
- Lets Cash Activity entries choose which cash account the movement belongs to.
- Lets Spending purchases use either a reward card or a cash account, so spending-account purchases can feed Insights without touching the bills-checking forecast.
- Shows estimated cash-account balances in the Overview money model.
- Fixes extra-income math so manual extra income is only counted once.
