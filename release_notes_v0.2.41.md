Pocket Ledger v0.2.41 Qt Preview

- Adds ledger type: Personal or Business.
- Adds per-ledger category management in Settings.
- Categories are now scoped to the active ledger, so Personal and business ledgers can have different category lists.
- Adds business category seeds such as Bookings, Square, Marketing, Advertising, Supplies, Software, Equipment, Travel, Contractors, Taxes, Fees, and Owner draw.
- Bill, Spending, Cash Activity, and Budget Target dialogs now use the active ledger's category list.
- Existing categories from older bills/spending/activity are backfilled into each ledger's category list.
- Exports now include category definitions.
