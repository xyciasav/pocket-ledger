Pocket Ledger v0.2.46 Qt Preview

- Adds Category Budget Health to Overview using monthly budget targets from Settings.
- Budget health now focuses on purchase spending by category, so recurring bills/debt do not distort variable spending budgets.
- Adds Credit Card Forecast to the Debt page.
- Card forecast estimates current card balance plus tracked card purchases this month plus upcoming card-paid bills tied to that card.
- Flags card forecast rows with green/orange/red based on remaining forecast room.
- Adds credit card forecast and variable budget health data to the local AI analysis payload.
