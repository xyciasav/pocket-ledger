Pocket Ledger v0.2.33 Qt Preview

- Adds a Cash Activity transfer action for moving money between cash accounts.
- Transfer creates money-out and money-in rows so each account balance stays understandable.
- Transfers are excluded from spending Insights so moving money between your own accounts does not look like spending.
- Cashflow now shows transfers for the bills-checking account when they affect that account.
- Direct spending from a cash account still belongs on Spending so categories and AI analysis can see what the money was used for.
