Pocket Ledger v0.2.42 Qt Preview

- Adds a Reimbursements page for business expenses you personally paid and need paid back for.
- Tracks reimbursement date, description, category, amount, paid-by, status, reimbursed date, notes, and receipt status.
- Adds reimbursement metrics for business owes you, open, submitted, reimbursed, and receipts attached.
- Adds actions to add/edit/delete reimbursements, mark reimbursed, attach a receipt, and open a saved receipt.
- Receipt attachments are copied into the local PocketLedger receipts folder so they stay private/local.
- Reimbursements now appear in Insights and the local AI data payload.
- Ledger CSV export now includes reimbursements and copies attached receipt files into the export folder.
